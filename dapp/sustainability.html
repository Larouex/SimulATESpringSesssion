Sustainability Statement

Our Commitment

We are thoughtful and committed to sustainability in our business, our actions and with our suppliers.

Larouex Gourmet Foods strive for following principles...

  • Committed to working locally to improve the social, economical and environmental well being of our community.
  • Committed to partnering with local area businesses, community groups and our neighbors to improve the place we live in and do business by implementing environmental projects that benefit our community socially and economically.
  • Minimise the use of paper wherever possible: for example, sending invoices and quotes via email instead of on paper.
  • Ensuring that all green and natural waste is disposed of according to industry standards and strong environmental principles.
  • Choose suppliers who promote sound environmental practices where possible.
  • Purchase products made with recycled paper.
  • Recycle as much waste material as possible.
  • Provide a safe and healthful workplace and working environments.
